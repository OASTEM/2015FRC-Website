The Team

This is our team's fourth FRC robot. This year, our team is sized at about twenty people. We draw in students from all grades, including both junior high and high schoolers. We have mentors from NASA JPL and Boeing, along with a parent mentor who works at HP. The team is generally split into three branches: Mechanical, Electrical, and Technology, though they are not completely locked down to stay in their branch. Everyone works together and helps wherever possible in order to build the robot. At the competition, we take these some members from the three main branches and add in some publicity and scouting teams so we have stronger bonds with other teams at the regional.
