Student Leads

  • Nathanael Shin

    FRC Lead

    As our FRC lead, he essentially responsible for the success or failure of our team. He plans the deadlines, the responsibilities of the group, and logistics such as food and transportation. Without his guidance, it would be difficult to be able to build a working robot if the basics are not out of the way first.

  • Byron Aguilar

    Electrical Lead

    Currently an 11th grader, Byron joined the FRC team early on in his high-school career. In addition to managing the wiring of the robot, he doubles as OA STEM's director of engineering. During the off season, he helps younger students learn the basics of engineering along with getting the team to work together on a VEX robot.

  • Lloyd Zhang

    Co-Mechanical Lead

    Lloyd is currently the member with the most years put into robotics, but as a senior, he is soon to graduate from this team. He was the director of Engineering in is sophomore year and continues to lead structurally sound designs, though occasionally he will use his "intuition" to put a few pieces together.

  • Aayush Patel

    Co-Mechanical Lead

    Aayush also helps out a lot with the mechanical ideas and implementation. He also doubles as the president of OA STEM, so he works behind the scenes in planning the logistics of not only the FRC competition, but various other events for the rest of the OA STEM team, which you can learn more about on the oastem tab.

  • Michael Duong

    Programming Lead

    Though it is only his second year of programming (Java is his primary language), his enthusiam and dedication to the team has allowed for him to gain experience quickly enough to guide himself and others through the programming of the robot. He started out with VEX programming earlier in the year and that experience has translated into beginning FRC programming.

  • Shelly Luong

    Publicity Lead

    Though she isn't an engineer or programmer, her enthusiastic dedication to the team goes towards the building of our team image. She works with OA STEM's Head of Publicity to organize publicity resources, such as shirts and stickers, and reaching out to others, whether it be through social media or face to face talking so when people hear of our team, it is only good things.
