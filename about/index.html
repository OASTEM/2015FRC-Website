Inception

Many years ago, our Robots Club emerged on our school campus. At the start, it was only a VEX and FLL based club, but over the years, thanks the the help of our mentors, we have begun to experience the wonders of FRC.

In 2013(?), members of the robotics club had a great idea: To create a STEM based club which brings opportunities to all branches STEM, not just engineering. We now have over 100 members and hope to continue growing, both in size, and success.
