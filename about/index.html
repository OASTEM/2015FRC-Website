History

This team started off purely as a Robotics/Engineering Club, often referred to as RobEn. At the time, VEX was their main robotic focus, but the club wanted to get bigger and try bigger things. In 2012, They entered FRC (Rebound Rumble) and changed RobEn to a full out STEM club, which you can read more about here.

This Website

Talk about having our very own FRC website was an idea that floated around for about two years. The subdomain frc.oastem.org was created on our web server, but for a very long time, it had literally nothing but the FRC logo. So clearly, the way forward was to let the guy who has no idea how web development works (but Java is clearly the same thing as HTML) to build an entire website from scratch.
